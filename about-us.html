About Us

Home About Us

Veeglobe Technologies is a leading IT & ITES (Information Technology Enabled Services) company that is based in Bangalore, India. Our core focus lies in providing next-generation software application, web application, and business process outsourcing (BPO) services to clients across the globe. Our innate passion is to forge successful relationships with clients worldwide and help them to build robust business processes and stay ahead of the competition through innovative technologies and business solutions.

Go beyond the traditional business partnership approach

We believe in building successful relationships. We are truly passionate about the success of our clients and ensure they are ready to meet next-gen demands in this challenging business landscape. At Vee Globe Technologies we don’t just sit around and ask for what needs to be done. In turn, we ask questions, find new opportunities, get feedback, and provide expertise on business-critical issues. For us it goes beyond just prices and processes, we are more interested in providing solutions that cater to the complex market demands and ensure our clients are ever ready for the change in market dynamics.

International Outsourcing Experience

Our business is an outsourcing model and our team closely works with various IT and ITES Clients across the globe. This gives us an edge over other similar players in the market, where we bring an international experience to our key personnel which translates into building exemplary products and services that meet global standards.

Highly-dedicated Team

Our highly-dedicated team provides you with unprecedented attention towards your services and ensures your business processes and applications are safe with us. We hire the best, and we ensure working with us can be a unique experience. We provide 24/7 support throughout the year, we solve your queries, respond quickly so that you do not miss out on any opportunities that are critical to profitability.

We Follow Globally-recognized Methodologies

We believe the structure of the organization plays a very important role in building next-gen technologies and handling the business-critical processes of our clients. Hence, we follow globally-recognized Project Management, IT Service Management and Quality Management methodologies to ensure we provide services that are of high quality, within budget and provide greater ROI to our clients worldwide.

Robust IP Protection

You can rest assured that your data is secure with us. We take both legal and physical measures to keep your data confidential and follow certain best practices to protect your intellectual property.
